Culture & Festivals of Maharashtra

Ganesh Chaturthi

A 10-day vibrant festival celebrating the birth of Lord Ganesha with processions, chants, and decorations.

Gudi Padwa

Maharashtrian New Year, celebrated with traditional flags, sweets like Puran Poli, and family gatherings.

Lavani Dance

A powerful and graceful folk dance performed to the beats of Dholki, showcasing Marathi heritage.
